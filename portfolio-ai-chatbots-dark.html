Challenges

TechCorp Middle East needed an intelligent chatbot solution to handle customer inquiries across multiple channels (website, WhatsApp, social media) in both Arabic and English. The existing support system was overwhelmed, with response times averaging 4-6 hours. They required a solution that could understand context, handle complex queries, and seamlessly escalate to human agents when needed.

  • Build multilingual AI chatbot supporting Arabic and English with natural language understanding.
  • Integrate with existing CRM and ticketing systems for seamless handoff to human agents.
  • Design conversational flows for common customer service scenarios in GCC markets.
  • Implement analytics dashboard to track conversation quality, resolution rates, and customer satisfaction.
  • Ensure GDPR and local data privacy compliance for GCC region.

    • Solutions

    We developed a custom AI chatbot using advanced NLP models trained on GCC-specific language patterns and business contexts. The solution integrates with WhatsApp Business API, website chat widgets, and social media platforms. The chatbot handles 85% of common inquiries automatically, reducing response time from hours to seconds. When escalation is needed, it seamlessly transfers context to human agents through the integrated CRM system. A comprehensive analytics dashboard provides real-time insights into conversation quality, customer satisfaction, and areas for improvement.
